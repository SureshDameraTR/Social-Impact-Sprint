## 2. Technical Architecture

### Addressing Ecosystem Shortfalls

The platform addresses four critical gaps in the rural livestock ecosystem:

| Shortfall | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Market Access Gaps** | Bridging market access gaps that hinder rural farmer profitability. |
| **Veterinary Shortages** | Addressing veterinary shortages through digital surveillance. |
| **Financial Exclusion** | Overcoming financial exclusion by building farmer credit history. |
| **Fodder Management Challenges** | Solving fodder management challenges for livestock health. |

### Data and Surveillance Challenges

- **Data Unavailability** — Providing localized data where none previously existed for livestock tracking.
- **Disease Surveillance** — Utilizing IoT to monitor health and prevent livestock outbreaks early.

### IoT Technical Integration

The platform integrates four IoT hardware systems:

1. **RFID Tags** — Deployment of RFID tags for unique livestock identification.
2. **Milk Meters** — Integration of milk meters to track dairy yield and quality.
3. **GPS Collars** — Real-time tracking using GPS collars for herd management.
4. **Smart Feeders** — Optimized nutrition through smart feeders and hatchery sensors.

### Integrated ERP Modules

| # | Module | Description |
|---|--------|-------------|
| 01 | **Cattle Management** | Comprehensive health and productivity tracking for dairy and beef cattle. |
| 02 | **Goats & Sheep** | Modules tailored for small ruminant health, leather, and meat production. |
| 03 | **Poultry Systems** | Advanced monitoring for egg production and hatchery management. |
| 04 | **Cross-Species Data** | Unified analytics platform for multi-species farming operations. |

### Multilingual Accessibility

- Support for **English, Hindi, Tamil, Kannada, Telugu, and Gujarati**.
- Seamless **voice-to-text** functionality for easier data entry.
- Intuitive **pictorial navigation** designed for low-literacy users.

### Offline Connectivity Solutions

Ensuring technology reaches the most remote locations through offline functionality. Designed for **low-connectivity villages**, the ERP functions offline and syncs data once a network is found, ensuring no farmer is left behind.

## UX Interface Reference

The mobile application consists of five primary screens:

### Screen 1: Home Dashboard ("My Animals & Earnings")
- Welcome greeting with search bar for animals and products
- Grid navigation with pictorial icons:
  - My Cattle
  - Goats/Sheep
  - Poultry
  - Sell Products
  - Animal Health
  - My Income
  - Vet Help
  - Alerts
- "Today's Summary" button at the bottom
- Bottom navigation tabs: Home, Animals, Sell, Income, Vet

### Screen 2: My Animals ("My Cows")
- List of registered cattle with individual cards:
  - Each card shows: animal name, today's milk yield (in liters), health status badge
  - Action buttons per animal: "Add Milk", "Health"
  - Example entries: Lakshmi (4.5 L, Good Health), Gauri (5.2 L, Good Health), Sundari (2.3 L, Vaccine Due)
- "+ Add New Cow" button at the bottom

### Screen 3: Sell Products ("Sell Today")
- "Quick sale to buyers nearby" subtitle
- Product grid with icons and prices:
  - Milk (Rs 40/L), Eggs (Rs 6/egg)
  - Goat/Sheep (Contact buyer), Manure (Rs 200/bag)
- Sell Milk form: price display (Rs 40/L), quantity selector (+/- stepper, showing 10 L), total earnings calculation (Rs 400)

### Screen 4: Income Tracker ("My Income")
- Weekly earnings summary card: Rs 4,200 with trend indicator (up 12% from last week)
- Breakdown by product:
  - Milk: Rs 3,200 (with progress bar)
  - Eggs: Rs 700
  - Manure: Rs 300
- Action buttons: "Download Record", "Apply for Loan"

### Screen 5: Vet Assistance ("Vet Help")
- "Get expert assistance" subtitle
- Contact options list:
  - Call Vet — Talk directly
  - Video Consult — Show your animal
  - Send Photo — Quick diagnosis
  - Send Voice Message — Explain the problem
- Emergency section (highlighted in red): "Emergency? Tap here for immediate help"
