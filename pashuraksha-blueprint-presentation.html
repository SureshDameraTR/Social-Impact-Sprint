The Problem

Why India's smallholder farmers need a digital lifeline

Current State

Paper Records, Zero Visibility

  • 80M+ smallholder livestock farmers in India, most own 2–10 animals
  • Health records are paper-based or non-existent — disease outbreaks go undetected
  • Milk collection relies on manual ledgers with no pricing transparency
  • Women-led SHG groups track savings in notebooks — no compliance visibility
  • Government subsidies go unclaimed due to documentation barriers

Farmer Experience

Design Principles

Built for Low-Literacy Users

  • Voice as PRIMARY input — not an add-on, the default interface
  • 64px+ pictographic icons — species, health status, actions
  • Color-coded severity: green/yellow/red for instant comprehension
  • Flat navigation: max 4 bottom tabs, max 2 taps to any screen
  • 48dp minimum touch targets (Material Design 3 built-in)
